Coquito

Description

Coquito is the puertorrican equivalent of eggnog, with a coconut twist. A delicious treat for the holidays!

Ingredients

  • 4 egg yolks, beaten
  • 3 cans evaporated milk
  • 1 can condensed milk
  • 1 can coconut cream
  • 3 cinnamon sticks
  • 1/2 t vanilla
  • Rum, optional and to taste
  • Ground cinnamon for serving, optional

Steps

  1. Blend first 5 ingredients, ideally with immersion blender.
  2. Add cinnamon sticks.
  3. Heat on medium heat, stirring constantly, until hot. Do not let boil.
  4. Remove from heat and let cool before adding vanilla and rum.
  5. Serve chilled, and sprinkle with cinnamon if desired.
