Mom's Bread Wreath

Description

This is the recipe my mom always brought to any gathering. It feeds a crowd, and is delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1 T yeast
  • 3 cups warm water
  • 1 T salt
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 c coconut oil
  • 1/4 c honey
  • flour

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 ยบ.
  2. Mix water and yeast, and let stand for ten minutes or until foamy.
  3. Wisk in eggs, salt, coconut oil and honey.
  4. Gradually add flour, mixing by hand when it becomes necessary, and knead into ball.
  5. Oil bowl, and add bread ball. Let rise for one hour.
  6. Punch down risen bread, and let rise for another hour.
  7. Divide bread dough into three, and roll each piece into a long piece. Braid together, and form wreath.
  8. Put bread into preheated oven, and bake for 40-50 minutes, until golden.
