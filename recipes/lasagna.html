Lasagna

Description

I love this lasagna recipe. The cream cheese gives it a creamy texture, and tastes much better than ricotta!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb cooked and seasoned ground beef
  • Your favorite tomato sauce
  • Cream cheese sauce, made with 8 z of cream cheese warmed with 1/4 c milk
  • Cooked lasagna noodles, or the precooked dry ones if you prefer
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 ยบ.
  2. Coat a 9x13 pan with a little sauce
  3. Layer the noodles, sauce, meat, and cream cheese mixture, starting and ending with noodles. Try to get at least two layers.
  4. Top with mozzarella.
  5. Bake 45 minutes, until bubbling and golden.
  6. Let stand 15 minutes before cutting.
